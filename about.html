About Lux Properties

Redefining real estate excellence across Nigeria since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lux Properties began with a simple vision: to transform the Nigerian real estate landscape by providing premium, transparent, and reliable property solutions. What started as a small consultancy in Lagos has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted real estate brands.

Over the past decade, we've facilitated over ₦500 billion in property transactions, helping thousands of Nigerians find their dream homes, secure profitable investments, and navigate the complex real estate market with confidence and ease.

Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction has earned us recognition as industry leaders and trusted partners in property transactions across major Nigerian cities.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional real estate services that exceed client expectations through innovation, integrity, and unparalleled expertise, making property transactions seamless and rewarding.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria's most trusted and innovative real estate partner, setting new standards in property services and creating lasting value for our clients and communities.
